1. Build Your Grid

Drag and drop any MP4 or WebM file (max 200MB) into the workspace. The engine will extract the frames into memory. Adjust columns and rows to define your mosaic.

2. Time Scrubbing

Click and drag horizontally on any tile to scrub through its timeline. Move left for the past, right for the future. You are literally painting with time.

3. Pin to Freeze

Right-click (or long-press on touch screens) to pin a tile. Pinned tiles get an amber border and are frozen in time. They will ignore all automated time presets.

4. Motion & Export

Use the Time Presets (Linear, Shuffle) to animate unpinned tiles. Set your Loop rules, hit the Preview button to see the magic, and Export as a high-res MP4/WebM.
